Google Calendar event images and flairs
A nice way to customize your calendar.

Did you know that Google calendar has secret background images for events? I didn't untill I created an event called "Lunch" and I saw the background image.

The background image I saw was the one above. These background images are also called flairs, and they can be applied to any event in any Google Calendar. To do this, you will need the right keywords for it to work. After some resarch and testing. I have made a list of each image, ID and what keywords you need to trigger the flair. They will only work when the keyword is in the title of the event.
